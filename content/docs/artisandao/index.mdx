title: Artisan DAO
description: Discover how Artisan ecosystem is orchestrated through its innovative DAO.

Artisan DAO is at the heart of Artisan Suite of products. As deep believers of the blockchain revolution 
and its benefits to reshape finance and governance, Artisan Labs wants its governance to leverage blockchain infrastructure, in order
to manage decision making, shareholding, as well as exchanges of Artisan Labs shares, in a smooth and efficient way.
