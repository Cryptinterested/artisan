**Initial issuance**: 
- **1,000,000 $ART** tokens ([ERC20](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-20) Standard) 
- New ART token minting events can happen in the future. They may corresponding to new fundraising events 
- Allocation: **800,000 $ART** distributed to Artisan Labs founding team, **200,000 $ART** distributed to early contributors and investors.
- The first 1,000,000 $ART will be subject to a lock-up period of two years, starting from the **01/03/2024**.
